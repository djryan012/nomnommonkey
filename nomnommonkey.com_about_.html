About

My name is Georgie, and while originally from the Bay Area, now reside in Orange County. Home of beautiful beaches and amazing sunsets! I met my husband in early 2012. We have two dogs, a scottie named MacKenzie and a boingle named Sable, a water turtle named Willie, a huge koi fish named Casper and 9 other smaller koi which I affectionately call the Jackson 5 because let’s face it, Tito had enough personality for 4 people!

Cooking, baking, the outdoors are among my favorite things and I look forward to our annual ski trip to Mammoth!! My Gramms inspired me with her love of cooking and I will share with you all the secret ingredient, LOVE! If you make it with love, love for yourself, for those you are cooking/baking for and just for the love of what you are doing, it will always come out amazing!

 

Welcome to my site

Welcome to NomNomMonkey! We all have busy schedules and don’t always have the time to whip up something gourmet. The food prepared is for my family on a daily basis, not at a restaurant and do not want to set a false expectation for what yours will look like. These are REAL recipes for REAL people just like you! You won’t see pictures with snazzy filters or food on china with silver utensils. So stay tuned, enjoy, and have fun!
